Event Discription

The Retro Art Fest is an annual celebration of vintage art styles, bringing together enthusiasts and retro artists from Malaysia and Indonesia. This vibrant event showcases a diverse range of retro art, including mid-century modern, art deco, pop art, and classic comic styles. Attendees can immerse themselves in a nostalgic atmosphere with live music and performances that capture the essence of the retro eras, adding to the festive atmosphere. There are also hands-on workshops led by skilled artists, where participants can learn techniques from different retro art movements. Food vendors offer a selection of food and drinks inspired by the culinary trends of the past, creating a full sensory experience. Additionally, a photo booth is available for attendees to capture their memories of the event.


Our Objective

  • Celebration of Historical Art and Culture
    • Highlighting art forms, styles, and cultural aspects from previous eras, allowing attendees to appreciate the creativity and design of the past.

  • Nostalgia and Entertainment
    • Offering a nostalgic experience for attendees, often through live performances, vintage markets, and interactive exhibits that recreate the ambiance of past decades.

  • Preservation of Cultural Heritage
    • Ensuring that the art and cultural practices of the past are preserved and appreciated by future generations, maintaining a connection to historical roots.
